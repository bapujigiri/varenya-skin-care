Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and treatments.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

During your initial consultation, our dermatologists will conduct a thorough skin analysis and discuss your concerns, goals, and medical history. Based on this assessment, we'll recommend the most appropriate treatments for your specific needs.

Is there any downtime after treatments?

Downtime varies depending on the treatment. Non-invasive procedures like facials typically have no downtime, while more intensive treatments like chemical peels or laser resurfacing may require a few days of recovery. We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions specific to your treatment.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your specific concerns and the treatment selected. For example, laser hair removal typically requires 6-8 sessions for optimal results, while some facials may show improvement after a single session. We'll create a personalized treatment plan with an estimated number of sessions during your consultation.

Are the treatments painful?

Most treatments are well-tolerated with minimal discomfort. We use advanced techniques and, when appropriate, topical anesthetics to ensure your comfort. Any sensations during treatment are typically brief, and we'll guide you through what to expect during your procedure.

Do you accept insurance for dermatology services?

We accept insurance for medically necessary dermatology services. Cosmetic procedures are typically not covered by insurance. Our billing department can verify your benefits and provide cost estimates before your treatment.
